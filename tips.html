How to pick the right products

It can sometimes be difficult to know which products will work for your hair, so, here are some tips to help you pick the right products.

    Know your hair type

    Not every product will work for your hair type, so, it's best you know your own hair type. There are many charts on the internet that you can use to determine your hair hair type, this is just one of many.

    3 Ingredients to avoid in your products

      Sulfates

      Sulfates strip away too much moisture, leaving the hair dry and unhealthy

      Alcohols

      Alcohols are drying and can cause damage to the hair.

      Parabens

      Parabens are easily absorbed through your skin and are harmful to your scalp. They cause drying, irritation and even hair loss.
